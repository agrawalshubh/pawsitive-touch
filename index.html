About Us

Welcome to Pawsitive Touch, your premier pet care destination in the heart of Model Town. We are a team of passionate pet lovers dedicated to providing the highest quality services and products for your beloved companions.

Our mission is to ensure the health, happiness, and well-being of every pet that comes through our doors. With years of experience and a genuine love for animals, we strive to create a welcoming and comfortable environment for both pets and their owners.
